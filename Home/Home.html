Brief History

The Putnam Area Robotics Team, formerly RoboGens, is located in Putnam County, WV. We are one of only four FRC teams in the state. Our team has grown this year from 8 students to nearly 30, and to include students from not only Winfield High (Generals), but Hurricane and Poca as well. Because of this, we voted to change our name from RoboGens to Putnam Area Robotics Team, or P.A.R.T.s We are headquartered at the Putnam Career and Technical Center in Eleanor, WV.

Our team strives to promote the First Robotics vision of engaging today's youth in the use of the latest science and technology. We hope to inspire future participation in FRC in our area by hosting free events where younger students can build and program Lego robots while gaining an understanding of teamwork
