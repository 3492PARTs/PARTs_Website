Interested in Joining P.A.R.T.s?

FIRST® FRC® Team 3492 P.A.R.T.s (Putnam Area Robotics Team) is a high school robotics team in the Wild and Wonderful State of West Virginia.

P.A.R.T.s 3492 is a team dedicated to inclusive opportunities in FIRST® Robotics Competition programs that educate and enrich youth in our communities with hands-on based advanced technology activities.

This is a varsity sport for the mind.
