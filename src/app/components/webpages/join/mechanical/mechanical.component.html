You could say that we are the builders of the skeleton of the robot. We lead the team in design and fabrication of the robot.

We look at the game mechanics and determine the optimal design for the robot. Our students and mentors work together to see what drive train we need, what mechanisms our robot needs, and anything else needed for the robot to score. Then, the design is drafted onto the computer with CAD software, and the physical specifications.
