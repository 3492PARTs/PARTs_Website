Who is Electrical?

The Electrical Team is responsible for the robot's control system. That being said, we are responsible for making sure that the control system is up to date and functioning properly.

Is Electrical Right for You?

If you work on electrical, you will need to learn now the systems on the robot work. We use Power Distribution Panels (PDPs), Bridges (WiFi Routers), Pneumatics Control Modules (PCMs), and other systems to run the robot. In Electrical, you don't work with just wires, but with pneumatic systems as well. Of course, we'll teach you everything you need to know. All that is required is a focused hardworking individual with a desire to learn.
