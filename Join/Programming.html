PROGRAMMING

WE are the PROGRAMMING Team

The PROGRAMMING team within PARTs is composed of a mentor and three students.

Brandon Duke is the programming mentor, currently a Computer Science student studying at Marshall University. Our current programming team is Jarren, a senior at Winfield High School; Jack, a sophomre at Hurricane High School; and Chance, a junior at Teays Valley Christian.

When it comes to programming we believe that anyone can do it. We take the time to teach any student reguardless of programming experience. For most of the students on the PROGRAMMING team now, started out with little or no experience what so ever. Over the years Brandon has developed training courses and labs to teach students, designed in mind for those who may be programming for the first time.

Programming is done in languages, much like speaking. Our language of choice is LabVIEW developed by National Instruments. LabVIEW is a graphical programing language perfect for those just learning. It allows students to see how the code works and walk through it, without the added stress of learning a text based programming language.

If you have any questions just email the team at TEAM3492@GMAIL.COM
