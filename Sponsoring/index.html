ABOUT FIRST & THE GAME

The Opportunity

To meet its financial needs, the team depends on corporate and business sponsors, personal donations, and fundraising events. The team has held events at local restaurants, sold items such as food and bumper stickers, and opened a GoFundMe account. Expenses for the team include the cost of robot components, event registration fees, travel expenses, hotels, meals, competition shirts, and other miscellaneous items (e.g., flags, signs, badges).

We greatly appreciate your consideration and support for our organization. We hope that you join us in continuing to build a strong team of intelligent, responsible, and motivated students.

Benefits of Being a Sponsor

  • Aiding in the development of a technologically literate workforce

  • Offering opportunities for the growth of team-building skills

  • Inspiring future programmers, engineers, and employees

  • Providing a source of pride in the community

  • Adding yet another positive facet to your corporate reputation

How to Sponsor

  • Donations Page

  • Contact Tina Kirk at (304) 552-4225 or Winfield High School at (304) 586-3279.

  • Send a check to Winfield High School. On the memo line, indicate that your donation is for 'Robotics'.

To help make the decision easier, please understand that as a 501(c)3 organization 100% of all donations will be utilized efficiently and effectively to support the Robotics Team. Also, all donations will be acknowledged in writing upon request.

Thank you for your support!
