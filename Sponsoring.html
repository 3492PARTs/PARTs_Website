About First & The Game

The Opportunity

There is a need for local sponsors to enable these students and mentors to not only build the robot, but to travel as a complete team to the competitions, which are 4 day, 3 night trips. Expenses include robot components, event registration fees, travel expenses, etc. We greatly appreciate your consideration and support for our organization and hope that you will join us in fulfilling our aspirations of continuing responsible, and personable students and citizens.

Benefits of Being a Sponsor

  • Aiding in the development of a technologically literate workforce
  • Offering opportunities for the growth of team-building skills
  • Inspiring future programmers, engineers, and employees
  • Providing a source of pride in the community
  • Adding yet another positive facet to your corporate reputation

How to Sponsor

Donations Page

See Donations Page or contact Tina Kirk at (304) 552-4225 or Winfield High School at (304) 586-3279. Please made checks payable to Winfield High School. On the memo line, indicate that your donation is for 'Robotics'. All donations will be acknowledged in writing including an official receipt for funds.

To help make the decision easier, please understand that as a 501(c)3 organization 100% of all donations will be utilized efficiently and effectively to support the Robotics Team.

Thank you for your support!
